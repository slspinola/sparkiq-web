FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is "plug-and-play" really in hours?

Partially true. Protocol-level auto-discovery (Sparkplug B Birth certificates, network scanning) works in minutes to hours. But complete business value - with ISA-95 contextualization, IEC 62443 security hardening, and MES/ERP integration - requires 4-12 weeks minimum.

Does it work with our 30-year-old legacy equipment?

Yes. Our edge gateways (EMQX Neuron, Ignition Edge, Litmus) support 450+ industrial protocols including Modbus RTU/TCP, PROFIBUS, PROFINET, S7, DeviceNet. If the equipment communicates via any protocol, we can connect it.

Is it safe for critical OT environments?

Absolutely. We strictly follow IEC 62443 standard with Zones & Conduits, TLS 1.3 encryption, granular RBAC, and safe scanning methods (TCP connect, not SYN scans). Claroty Edge performs discovery in <10 min with zero operational impact.

What's the typical ROI and timeline?

Customers report: 15-20% OEE improvement, 30-40% downtime reduction, 35-50% faster troubleshooting. Typical ROI in 12-18 months. MVP in 4-12 weeks ($60-180k), production in 3-6 months ($240-600k).

How does the AI/LLM layer work?

LLMs subscribe to UNS, perform data quality assurance, noise cleaning, anomaly detection, and predictive maintenance. Hybrid deployment: Llama 3.2 3B on edge (10-100ms latency), Llama 70B on-premise, GPT-4 cloud for complex analysis. RAG with technical manuals ensures zero hallucinations.
